Personal Introduction

Interest & Passions

Would it be able for us to envision the future of brain science decades later — How can the next generation of brand-new neurotechnology profit to the industries in brain-computer-interface, and how can the scientific progress in the neuroscience bring hope to the people afflicted with brain disease?

Even out of the long-term academic curiosity, I could never stop asking: Why can our brain give rise to its incredible intelligence in cognition levels upon the observation and the perception?

I am interested in learning of the secrets of the brain: how to play the role of the “operating systems” equipped in our humans; I incline to the ideation in which leveraging the computational methodology to simulate specific neural capabilities, and unraveling the mathematical model behind specific brain functions.

However, I have no gift to be a computer or mathematics geek. Knowing the contributions I could make across my whole life might be the very tip of the iceberg, I wish my modest means could one day be amplified via collective efforts from the like-minded people who as well bears passions on better understanding our brains.
